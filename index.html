Privacy Policy for Pico Habit

Effective Date: 26th January 2025

Pico Habit (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or information that you enter into the app. Any information you provide through free text fields or other features remains stored locally on your device.

Data Usage

Any data you enter into Pico Habit is used solely by you and is not transmitted to us or any third-party servers. The app does not include any mechanisms to collect, upload, or share the data you enter.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect user data, we do not share or sell any personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Pico Habit does not use third-party services (e.g., analytics tools, advertising platforms) that may collect or process user data.

User Responsibility

As your data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for managing and securing this data. We recommend using any available security features on your device (e.g., passcodes, encryption) to protect your data.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Any data entered by children remains local to their device.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If changes are made, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy.
